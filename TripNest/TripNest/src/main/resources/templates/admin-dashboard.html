Frequently Asked Questions
How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the Settings page and click on the "Security" tab. From there, you can change your password by following the on-screen instructions.
How can I add a new user?
You can add a new user by clicking on the "Add User" button in the Quick Actions section or by navigating to the Users page and clicking the "Add New User" button.
Where can I view my reports?
Reports can be accessed by clicking on the "Reports" option in the Quick Actions section or by navigating to the Analytics page from the sidebar.
How do I export my data?
To export your data, go to the Reports section and use the export options available at the top of each report. You can export data in CSV, PDF, or Excel formats.
